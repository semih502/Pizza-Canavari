First of all, I would like to highlight that everything you see here was built in exactly one month. This is the first Unreal Engine project for both myself and my teammates in the industry. We would greatly appreciate any feedback regarding bugs or other issues you may encounter.

Controls:

Move: "W", "A", "S", "D"

Sprint: "Shift"

Crouch: "Ctrl"

Jump & Climb: "Space"

Interact: Press "E" to talk to NPCs and discover the story.

Additional details and lore are available through dialogues with NPCs throughout the game. We hope you enjoy it!
